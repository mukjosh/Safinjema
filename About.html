Who We Are

Cleaning with Care & Conscience

SaFi Njema — Swahili for "Clean Well" — is a professional eco-friendly cleaning company based in Cape Town. We provide reliable residential, commercial and industrial cleaning designed to keep every space spotless and hygienic.

Our trained specialists use modern equipment and environmentally-certified products to ensure high-quality cleaning while protecting your health and the environment.
